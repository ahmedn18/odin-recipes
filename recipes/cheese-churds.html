Real Wisconsin Fried Cheese Curds

Real Wisconsin cheese curds! Battered and fried to golden perfection.

Ingredients:

  • 2 quarts corn oil for frying
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup beer
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 pounds cheese curds, broken apart

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients. Heat corn oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Whisk together flour, beer, milk, eggs, and salt to form a smooth, rather thin batter.
  3. Place cheese curds, about 6 or 8 at a time, into the batter, stir to coat, and remove with a wire strainer.
  4. Shake the curds a time or two to remove excess batter.
  5. Deep fry curds in hot oil until golden brown, 1 or 2 minutes. Drain on paper towels; serve hot.
